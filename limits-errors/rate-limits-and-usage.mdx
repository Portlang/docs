---
title: "Rate Limits & Usage"
---

To ensure fair usage and system stability, HS Ping's rate limit varies by plan. Please refer to table below for rate limit of each plan.

| **Plan** | **Rate limit** |
| --- | --- |
| Free | 50 lookup / minutes |
| Professional | 100 lookup / minutes |
| Volume | 200 lookup / minutes |
| Scale | 500 lookup / minutes |

## Monthly Free Quota

All users (free and pay) have access to our monthly free quota at 100 lookups per month.

This quota resets every 24 hours at 12am UTC.
